Explore Popular Places in India

Taj Mahal, Agra

One of the Seven Wonders of the World, known for its stunning marble architecture.

Price: ₹1000 per entry

Nearby Hotels: Oberoi Amarvilas, ITC Mughal

Golden Temple, Amritsar

The holiest gurdwara of Sikhism, known for its golden dome and serene ambiance.

Price: Free entry

Nearby Hotels: Hyatt Regency, Ramada Amritsar

Hawa Mahal, Jaipur

A unique palace with a distinctive honeycomb structure, known as the "Palace of Winds".

Price: ₹200 per entry

Nearby Hotels: Jai Mahal Palace, Trident Jaipur

Mysore Palace, Mysore

A historical palace known for its grand architecture and royal heritage.

Price: ₹70 per entry

Nearby Hotels: Radisson Blu, Royal Orchid Metropole

Marine Drive, Mumbai

A picturesque coastal road known as the "Queen's Necklace" for its stunning night view.

Price: Free entry

Nearby Hotels: The Taj Mahal Palace, Trident Nariman Point

Kerala Backwaters

Serene waterways and houseboats offering a unique way to explore Kerala.

Price: ₹1500 per boat ride

Nearby Hotels: Kumarakom Lake Resort, Houseboat stays

Khajuraho Temples, Madhya Pradesh

Famous for their erotic sculptures and stunning architecture.

Price: ₹600 per entry

Nearby Hotels: Lalit Temple View, Radisson Jass

Charminar, Hyderabad

An iconic mosque known for its four grand minarets and Indo-Islamic architecture.

Price: ₹50 per entry

Nearby Hotels: Taj Krishna, ITC Kakatiya

Red Fort, Delhi

A historic fort that served as the main residence of Mughal emperors.

Price: ₹500 per entry

Nearby Hotels: The Imperial, Le Meridien

Sundarbans, West Bengal

Largest mangrove forest in the world, home to the Royal Bengal Tiger.

Price: ₹200 per entry

Nearby Hotels: Sundarban Tiger Camp, Sundarban Jungle Camp

Andaman Beaches

Pristine beaches known for their crystal-clear waters and coral reefs.

Price: Free entry

Nearby Hotels: Taj Exotica, SeaShell Resort

Lakshadweep Islands

A group of islands known for their beautiful lagoons and diving spots.

Price: ₹500 per permit

Nearby Hotels: Agatti Island Beach Resort, Bangaram Island Resort

Rann of Kutch, Gujarat

The vast white salt desert, famous for the Rann Utsav and cultural heritage.

Price: ₹100 per entry

Nearby Hotels: Rann Riders, Shaam-e-Sarhad Village Resort

Umaid Bhawan Palace, Jodhpur

A grand palace showcasing royal history, part of which is a luxury hotel.

Price: ₹300 per entry

Nearby Hotels: Umaid Bhawan Palace Hotel, Ajit Bhawan

Jaisalmer Fort, Rajasthan

Known as the "Golden Fort", it is one of the largest fully preserved fortified cities in the world.

Price: ₹250 per entry

Nearby Hotels: Suryagarh, Desert Tulip Hotel

Konark Sun Temple, Odisha

A 13th-century temple dedicated to the sun god, known for its unique chariot-like structure.

Price: ₹600 per entry

Nearby Hotels: Lotus Eco Resort, Mayfair Waves

Ajanta Caves, Maharashtra

Ancient rock-cut caves featuring Buddhist art and architecture from the 2nd century BCE.

Price: ₹600 per entry

Nearby Hotels: Ajanta T Junction Hotel, Kailas Hotel

Brihadeeswarar Temple, Tamil Nadu

A UNESCO World Heritage site, known for its massive dome and intricate carvings.

Price: ₹500 per entry

Nearby Hotels: Ideal River View Resort, Svatma Hotel

Qutub Minar, Delhi

The tallest brick minaret in the world, an important symbol of India's medieval history.

Price: ₹500 per entry

Nearby Hotels: The Oberoi, The Ashok

Ellora Caves, Maharashtra

A UNESCO World Heritage site known for its monumental rock-cut temples and artwork.

Price: ₹600 per entry

Nearby Hotels: Hotel Kailas, Ellora Heritage Resort
